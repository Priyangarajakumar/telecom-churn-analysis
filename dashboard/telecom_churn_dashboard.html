Top 3 Churn Drivers

  1. Month-to-month contracts with fiber optic internet and no online security.
  2. Early-tenure customers in the first 12 months of service.
  3. Customers paying via manual and high-friction methods such as electronic checks.

Churn Prevention Strategy Framework

  • Financial Interventions: Targeted discounts or loyalty pricing for high-risk month-to-month and high-charge customers.
  • Service Improvements: Bundle and promote online security and tech support for fiber and high-usage customers.
  • Engagement Improvements: Proactive onboarding, education, and outreach in the first 12 months, with focus on manual payers to move them to autopay.
